To the love of my life,

I was just thinking back to when we first met at OPM during the pandemic. Looking back, those four years of being best friends were the foundation for everything. I never would have guessed that the person I shared so many laughs and stories with back then would be the person I'm celebrating an anniversary with today.

Happy 1st Anniversary, my love. I can't wait for everything that's coming next for us.

Forever yours,
